About Event

GEEKATHON'18 is a student-held coding contest organised by Programming Club OGI in the month of february. It aims to encourage the coders to think innovatively by stepping out of the customary realm of textbooks,and stepping into the functional, application based domain. As an initiative taken to provide a plethora of opportunities to all the creative minds working in technical domain, GEEKATHON'18 inspires them to articulate solutions of problems in a given time refrain. We, at Programming Club aim to provide a platform for students to come together and participate actively in tech-savvy sessions and discover the pool of underlying possibiities. It started as an insightful idea of few enthusiastic students of Oriental Group of Institutes, Bhopal. Revolving around the central idea of competitive coding, this time we are bringing GEEKATHON'18.
